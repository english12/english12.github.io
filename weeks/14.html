Week 14: Business as usual

Monday: Day 64

The first part of class was spent assigning vocab words. Then, after lunch, we went back to the hallway to do “Shakespeare in the Round” because Ms. Clark liked the hallway better than the auditorium. We finished reading act 4 and started act 5. #vocab

Tuesday: Day 65

We went right away to the computer lab to “work on our term papers”. #library

Wednesday: Day 66

Oh boy: Hamlet. First, the act 5 group finished reading act 5 of Hamlet. Then we finished the movie of Hamlet. Then we quickly discussed Hamlet. #movie

Thursday: Day 67

We went to the computer lab to work on our papers again and for the whole time. #library

Friday: Day 68

After our new tradition of vocab stories before lunch, we got a Hamlet packet that we worked on the whole time and did not finish. #vocab
