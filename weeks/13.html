Week 13: The films II

Wednesday: Day 61

Ms. Clark kicked off today’s class by telling us about the Globe Theatre and handing out a nice little sheet that had obviously been copied one too many times. Then we watched Hamlet. Then we went to lunch. Then we watched Hamlet. Then, we desperately tried to gain entrance into the auditorium to do act four of Hamlet [I don’t know if we ever finished act three]. That didn’t work, so the presenting group “acted” in the hallway. No sleeping :-( #movie

Thursday: Day 62

Today was outline day. We went to the computer lab and worked on our thorough outlines. #library

Friday: Day 63

It’s Friday again. Once again, we didn’t have time to study before lunch, so we just wrote our ‘stories’ with the vocab list and the definitions. After lunch, we watched a film of a performance of The Complete Works of William Shakespeare, condensed or something. It was all of Shakespeare presented in a comedic fashion. #vocab #movie
