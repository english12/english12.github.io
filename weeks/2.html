Week 2: The films I

Monday: Day Four

We spent the first part of class picking vocab and moving to the library. After lunch, I worked on my poster [1]. I did not finish it, even though the poster is due tomorrow. Anyways, I was promised some more time to work on it, so I’m not worried. #vocab #movie

Wednesday: Day Six

We arrived in English with the promise of a movie. Ms. Clark spent a good deal of time messing around with the VHS that obviously didn’t work because the sound kept going in and out. This fiddling lasted until lunch during which she got a DVD version (!!). After lunch we watched the ‘film’, Camelot, and answered questions/notes on a sheet. #movie

Thursday: Day Seven

First, we went over the questions that I never received because I missed Tuesday [2]. One person from each “group” was supposed to say the answer, but basically she just told us the answers. I had to write really fast. After lunch, we finished up with that, and we watched a movie about King Arthur. Another one. #movie

Friday: Day Eight

Upon entering the room, it was immediately evident that we had a sub today. For the before-lunch part of class, we “studied our vocab”. After lunch, we took our vocab quiz. Then we finished the movie. No one knew where we left off because no one was paying that much attention. We settled on a point 7 minutes from the end. After the movie concluded, we headed for the library to research our King Arthur (and/or Grendel) research topics. #sub #vocab #movie #library

Notes

[1] I don’t know what this “poster” was for.

[2] I was sick
