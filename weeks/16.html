Week 16: One out of fifteen

Monday: Day 73

Today, before lunch, we finished up our fun plague movie. Then we were given more reading assignment :-( #movie

Tuesday: Day 74

Today, we started off the period by trying to tackle the enormous list of short stories and essays she handed out yesterday. Of fifteen essays, [which were supposed to have been started yesterday] we almost started reading the first. After lunch, we went to the library to work on the stories (nope), plan our Hamlet project (“yes”), or finish our rough draft for our term papers (due last Friday). I should explain the Hamlet project. She handed out a poorly-formatted list of project ideas, including “grab bag with 10 objects”, “write a play”, “power point presentation”, and more. Our group chose “do a movie.” We sat in the library for the rest of the period. #library

Wednesday: Day 75

For the part before lunch, Ms. Clark (and various student helpers [1]) handed back graded work. This was mostly composed of vocab stories. Then, we went to the library to work on the list of short stories. After three days of work, we are almost done with the first of fifteen. #library

Thursday: Day 76

You never told us we had to read it The class

In a particularly funny first part of class, Ms. Clark handed out a slip of paper with questions about “The Demon Lover”. Then she announced it was a quiz. The room performed an uproar, as no one had read it in spite of the ~three days of class time we had to do it. “You never told us we had to read it,” we complained. In the end, she just told us the answers to the four questions and it was not a quiz. But we better read it tonight, because she might give us the quiz again “tamarrow” [2]. After lunch, we went to the library to work on our projects, rough drafts (mind you, the final copy is due tomorrow), or the readings. Ha! #library

Friday: Day 77

First, we spent the usual lot of time collecting final drafts of our literary analyses. Then we started watching Gulliver’s Travels movie. Then we continued watching Gulliver’s Travels movie. #movie
