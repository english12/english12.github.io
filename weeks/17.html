Week 17: Denouement

Monday: Day 78

This period is divided into four parts. We’re going to read for two and go to the library for the third.

Much better than last Friday when she told us what we were going to do for the “second two thirds”. We were supposed to be reading some essays. We did not. Then we went to the library and hung out. #library

Post-Mortem

And just like that, the log ended. I will never know for sure why I didn’t write a grandiose swelling conclusion about the last day of classes. Probably because I couldn’t be bothered. Maybe because English 12 didn’t deserve a concluding paragraph.

I’m sure ms. Clark continued her nonsense-based film study of Old England for more years to come. There might be students blowing off a vocab quiz this very moment.

You can try to find someone to hold accountable: The administration, unions, Ms. Clark herself. Maybe there was just too much fluorine in the water supply. The placement of blame doesn’t matter. The only thing that matters is we left that class with a very important realization: English 12. What a waste of time.
