Week 12: The performances

Intermission / editor’s note

The story takes a turn after week 11. That must have been my breaking point. Up until then, the absurdity was fun. I’d describe the day’s details with glee. There’s only so much glee a second-semester senior has to give. From this point, the glee gives way to drudgery. The log becomes a chore. The absurdity isn’t worth it anymore. We all wish Ms. Clark followed her baby calf off the cliff.

Monday: Day 54

Straight into class, we went straight into the auditorium. The average student spent most of his/her time:

  • Sleeping
  • Talking
  • Texting
  • Day Dreaming

All while several students were on stage unenthusiastically reading their act of Hamlet. The best part was every once in a while, Ms. Clark would get up out of her chair, go into the aisle, look/squint at the presenters in a very interested manner, and nod before sitting back down again. A little movie was watched at the end, along with picking vocab. #vocab #movie

Tuesday: Day 55

Before lunch we watched some movie. After that, we did yesterday again with a new group and a different act. #movie

Wednesday: Day 56

We came in to the room to find Ms. Clark not there. The sub plans called for us to watch Hamlet before lunch and go to the library after lunch to work on term paper notecards. Once the movie was put on, all attention-paying ceased. Everyone was just talking to one another and generally chilling. After lunch, the library was full of AP testing, so we continued to watch—excuse me, “watch” the movie. Then we went to the library for a bit to do our notecards. #sub #movie #library #library

Thursday: Day 57

Today we had a messed up sub. She glared at people who were talking, and told them to face forward. Everyone was just like, “yeah whateva”. She made us an offer: “if and only if” we all discuss Frankenstein, we can do it as a class and not have to write it down. The discussion that followed was embarrassing. After lunch we watched Frankenstein the movie, which was hilarious. #sub #movie

Friday: Day 58

Shocker!: We did vocab before lunch, once again with the choice to write a story on the computer given the words and definitions. After lunch we finished act 2 and started act 3 in the auditorium. Brilliant! #vocab
